📋 The Rules, Girlie

Our Sacred Bylaws 📜

01

🚫 NO CIS FEMALES 🚫

This is a club for trans women. Not women who "support trans women." Not allies. Not curious cis girls. Trans. Women. Only. Sis, if you have to ask whether you qualify — the answer is probably yes, actually. But if you're cis, this isn't your space. We love you. Go find your own thing. 💖

02

💸 Only Solana Girlie Pops

We trade Solana Network spot assets. Not futures. Not leverage. Spot. High-risk is the vibe but we're not here to get liquidated into debt, babe. 📉

03

✨ Share the Alpha

Good calls go in the group chat. We rise together. Hoarding info is not the girlie way. 📲

04

🏳️‍⚧️ Trans Rights Always

This club is explicitly, loudly, and unapologetically pro-trans-rights. No exceptions, no "both sides," no debate. 🌈

05

🌸 Brooklyn Code

Brooklyn-based meetups. We do IRL sessions. Cafes, parks, rooftops. Wherever girlies congregate. 🗽

06

💅 No Judgment on Bags

Whether you're trading $50 or $50,000, you're one of us. We're all building. We don't do class snobbery here. Money is a tool, not a personality. 💎

📊 What We Trade

Solana or Nothing, Girlypop 🔮

We are Solana maximalists. We are also not financial advisors. We are trans women in Brooklyn who figured out that the Solana ecosystem slaps and that high-risk spot trading is a legitimate path to funding hormones, surgeries, closets full of vintage Y2K fashion, and full autonomy over our own bodies and lives.
⚡

Solana Network

Fast. Cheap. Volatile. Perfect for girlies who don't have time for slow chains and $50 gas fees. SOL moves, and so do we. 🏃‍♀️

💎

Spot Trading Only

We trade what we own. Spot positions. No leverage meltdowns. High risk comes from the assets themselves — we don't need to amplify it artificially. 📈

Our Style 💅

Memecoins & Alts

We go deep into the Solana ecosystem. SPL tokens, memecoins, ecosystem plays. If it's on Solana and it moves, we're watching it. 👀

🫂

Group Alpha Sessions

Weekly(ish) meetups — virtual and IRL in Brooklyn — where we share charts, calls, and emotional support for when the market is not being our friend. 💔→💰

🧠

On-Chain Research

We study Solana on-chain data together. Wallet tracking, DEX flows, token launches. We are not degen randos. We are informed degen randos. 🔬

Self-Custody Only

We hold our own keys. Not your keys, not your coins. We keep our bags safe because nobody else is going to do it for us. 🗝️

📚 Financial Education

Build Your Wealth
For Girlie Things 💸✨

Like surgery. And clothes. And rent. And freedom. And all the things. 🌸
🏦

Crypto Basics for Girlies

Starting from zero? Amazing. We cover wallets, seed phrases, DEXs, CEXs, and why you should never keep your whole bag on an exchange. No judgment. Everyone starts somewhere. 🌱

📊

Reading Charts Without Crying

Candlestick charts are not that deep. RSI, MACD, support/resistance — we make it make sense without the weird finance bro energy. We use pink highlighters. ✏️

🌺

Funding Your Transition

This is the whole point tbh. We share resources on how to allocate trading profits toward HRT, surgery funds, gender-affirming care, and everything in between. 🩷

💰

DeFi & On-Chain Yield

Your money can work while you sleep and get your nails done. We cover Solana DeFi protocols, LP strategies, and staking — in plain English. 💅

🛡️

Risk Management, Babe

Position sizing. Stop losses. When to take profits. The unsexy but absolutely critical stuff that keeps you in the game long enough to actually win. 🎯

🌍

Tax & Legal for Crypto

Crypto taxes are real and annoying. We share what we know (NOT tax advice, girlies) and point you toward real resources that won't let the IRS ruin your day. 📝
